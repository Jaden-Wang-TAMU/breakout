Breakout!

Proceed through the five levels by breaking all 64 bricks of each level!

The 1st level is standard, the 2nd level has smaller bricks, the 3rd level has a smaller paddle, the 4th level has a smaller ball, and the 5th level turns your paddle invisible!

You start with 10 lives, and each brick is worth different points! Yellow=1, Green=3, Orange=5, Red=7. There are also 2 random explosive and teleporter bricks per level!

Here are your power-up blocks: The teal block lets you teleport the ball, the maroon block lets you move the paddle more, and the blue block creates another ball!

You will receive alerts whenever you hit special bricks or power-up blocks. Right click to turn off alerts, and right click again to turn them on again!
